HOW TO USE

Placing Bodies

  • Click anywhere to place a small planet
  • Click & Drag to slingshot a planet with velocity
  • Right-click to place a massive star

🌀 Orbit Setup Mode

Click the Orbit Setup button, then:
Step 1: Click to place a central star.
Step 2: Move your mouse to set the orbit radius — a preview circle shows the path. Click to launch a planet in a mathematically perfect circular orbit.

Invisible Interactions

Every body emits an invisible gravitational field (shown as expanding ripple rings). When two bodies are close enough, an Invisible Bond forms — a flowing energy line connecting them. The glowing dot at the midpoint is the Force Nexus, where the interaction peaks. Small arrows (whiskers) on each body reveal the direction of the hidden gravitational pull.

The Cosmic Song

Enable audio to hear gravity turned into music. Each pair of interacting bodies produces a tone — the closer two bodies are, the more consonant the interval (Perfect 5th, Major 3rd). As they drift apart, the interval becomes more dissonant (Tritone, Minor 2nd). The result is a unique symphony shaped entirely by orbital geometry.

Hover

Hover your mouse over any body to see its mass, velocity, kinetic energy, and more.

Controls

  • ♫ Start Audio — Enable/disable the gravitational symphony
  • Trails — Toggle orbital trail lines
  • + Random — Add a random body near the center
  • Reset — Clear all bodies
